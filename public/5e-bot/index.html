Why use a 5e bot in Discord instead of separate references?

Context switching is what slows a game down. If the party needs to confirm a spell description, check a monster detail, or answer a rules question, it is faster to resolve that in Discord than it is to send everyone to separate tabs and external notes.

  • Useful during live sessions when the DM needs fast rulings
  • Good for play-by-post servers where searchable references stay in-channel
  • Pairs naturally with the bot's dice and initiative commands

FAQ

Does 5e-Bot only cover dice rolls?

No. It includes dice rolling, but the 5e lookup commands are a major part of the bot and cover a wide range of SRD categories.

Can I search spells and monsters directly in Discord?

Yes. The bot includes dedicated commands for spell and monster lookups, plus broader commands for other 5e categories.

What is `/query` for?

`/query` provides AI-assisted rules answers for 5e questions when the table needs a fast explanation or summary.
