What a good D&D bot for Discord should cover

When people search for a Discord Dungeons & Dragons bot, they are usually trying to solve practical problems during play. A useful bot should help with table mechanics, reduce channel clutter, and return rules information quickly enough to keep the game moving.

  • Fast dice input for checks, attacks, damage, and ad hoc rolls
  • Combat commands that keep initiative visible and easy to advance
  • Reliable 5e SRD search for spells, monsters, classes, items, and conditions
  • Simple enough commands that new players can use them with minimal setup

FAQ

Is this just a dice roller?

No. Dice rolling is one part of the bot, but it also includes initiative commands and 5e SRD lookup tools.

Can it help a DM during combat?

Yes. The initiative commands are meant for encounter management, and the search commands help with fast lookups while combat is running.

Does it work for 5e specifically?

Yes. 5e-Bot focuses on Dungeons & Dragons 5e content and commands that are useful for 5e tables in Discord.
